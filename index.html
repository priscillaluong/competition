About us

Baxters was established in 2005 under the creative vision of Darren Baxter. Based in London, Baxters creates carefully selected, beautiful floral displays. We have worked with various clients that excel in their respective field, including Val Garland, Sam McKnight, Mary McCartney, Gucci, Clarins and Whistles.



About the founder

With a bachelors in Fashion Design at London College of Fashion, Darren Baxter has an eye for detail and a vision for perfection. As a child, Baxter has always found the world of flora and fauna a fascinating focal point in his artwork. After graduating, Baxter worked as a creative designer and, over the next 7 years, would go on to work in Paris, Milan and Shanghai. Flowers were regularly incorporated into Baxter's designs, but it wasn't until he was promoted to Creative Director at a prominant fashion house in London that Darren Baxter finally realised where his life was leading. He left the fashion industry and turned his talents towards the world of floral design. Crafting luscious bouquets and show-stopping displays, it is no surprise that Baxter has built a reputation among the creative industry's upper echelon.
